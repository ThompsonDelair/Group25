Consumers in Metro Vancouver generate of 100,000 tonnes of avoidable food waste each year. Our web application, Don't Be Spoiled, is a tool that will help educate people about how to store their food. We believe that by providing easy storage tips and building a community that supports one another, food waste can be reduced.
